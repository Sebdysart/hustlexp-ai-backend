Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 14, 2026 · Last Updated: February 14, 2026

Summary: HustleXP collects only the data needed to connect you with local tasks, process payments, and improve the app. We never sell your personal information to third parties.

1. Introduction

HustleXP ("we," "our," or "us") operates the HustleXP mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By downloading, accessing, or using HustleXP, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Match you with relevant local tasks based on your location and preferences
  • Process payments and payouts between task posters and Hustlers
  • Calculate and display XP, levels, and user ratings
  • Send push notifications about task updates, messages, and account activity
  • Provide customer support
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, or security issues
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the App's features and performance
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We only share data as described above or when required by law.

We may also share information in the following circumstances:

  • Legal requirements: When required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request
  • Safety: To protect the rights, safety, or property of HustleXP, our users, or others
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (you will be notified of any change in ownership)

5. Location Data

HustleXP uses your device's location to match you with nearby tasks and display distance estimates. Location access is requested through your device's standard permissions prompt.

You can control location access at any time through your iOS Settings. Disabling location services will limit the App's ability to show nearby tasks.

6. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers provided by Neon (database) and Railway (API hosting), both located in the United States. We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication via Firebase
  • Payment data handled exclusively by Stripe's PCI-compliant infrastructure
  • Access controls limiting who can view user data internally

No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. Specifically:

  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Task history & XP data: Retained while your account is active for leaderboard and reputation features
  • Payment records: Retained as required by tax and financial regulations (typically 7 years)
  • Crash logs: Automatically deleted after 90 days

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Data portability — request your data in a commonly used, machine-readable format
  • Opt out of notifications — manage push notification preferences in your device settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@hustlexp.app. We will respond within 30 days.

9. California Residents

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides additional rights:

  • The right to know what personal information is collected, used, shared, or sold
  • The right to delete personal information held by businesses
  • The right to opt out of the sale of personal information — we do not sell your data
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your CCPA rights

10. Cookies & Analytics

HustleXP is a native iOS application and does not use browser cookies. However, we use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized usage data (such as screen views, feature usage, and session duration) to improve the App. This data is not linked to your personal identity.

If you access HustleXP web pages (such as this Privacy Policy), standard web server logs may record your IP address and browser information.

11. Children's Privacy

HustleXP is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we learn that we have collected data from a person under 18, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us at privacy@hustlexp.app.

12. Beta Testing

HustleXP is currently in private beta. During the beta period, we may collect additional diagnostic data (such as detailed usage logs and feedback submissions) to improve the App before public release. Beta participants will be notified of any data collection specific to the testing program.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify you through the App or by email before the changes take effect. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

Your continued use of HustleXP after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
